Mô tả

The Gios Compact Pro Evolution is an example of exquisite steel frames. Made of lugged Cro-Mo steel tubing - specifically designed for this Gios - this model is a real thoroughbred racer.

To make the geometry of the frame more versatile, the adjustable rear dropouts were installed - a pioneering invention by the legendary brand from Torino.


Our Gios Compact Evolution keeps the original paintwork - the iconic "Gios Blue" - and is fully equipped with Campagnolo parts. From Record Ergopower 8-speed shifters to Chorus cranks, brakes, headset or seatpost.
